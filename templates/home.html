Priortizing the good sleep is good self-care..

Sleep trackers are devices used to measure sleep quality. Sleep trackers measure how long you have slept, the depth of your sleep, and other sleeping habits. Sleep trackers can be valuable in gaining insight into how much sleep you are getting and, as a result, making lifestyle changes to optimise your sleeping experience.

About the Project

The purpose of this project is to design and develop a wearable device for sleep quality measurement. The project would help evolve a product that can help in the measurement of sleep quality on a more realistic scale.

It promotes awareness of the importance of sleep that can strengthen physical and mental health of an individual.

Factors affecting sleep quality:
-> Digital Display: body temperature, heart rate, SPO2
-> Output in the app integrated via bluetooth: In addition to above factors - surrounding temperature, light, activity index, direction, atmospheric pressure, rest and wake periods will be reported

This is how a user could develop sleep plan and improve one's sleep quality. The webapp would suggest improvements to the user based on final output.
